How we helped create an AI-powered construction management platform that revolutionizes workflows for builders and trade partners

Tech Stack

The Challenge

ViusBuilt approached us with a vision to transform the construction industry's outdated workflows. Construction companies were struggling with:

  • Manual paperwork consuming 20+ hours weekly
  • Compliance tracking across multiple trade partners
  • Inefficient bid request and estimation processes
  • Lack of real-time project visibility
  • Disconnected communication between builders and subcontractors

Our Solution

We developed a comprehensive AI-powered construction management platform that includes:

AI Document Verification

Automated compliance tracking and document verification using machine learning algorithms

Smart Bid Management

Streamlined bid request system that reduced submission time by 50%

Real-time Collaboration

Instant updates and communication between builders and trade partners

QuickBooks Integration

Seamless financial management with automated invoicing and payment tracking
