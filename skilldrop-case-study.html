SkillDrop Case Study

Creating a global educational marketplace that connects expert instructors with learners across Russia and beyond

Project Overview

Client

Industry

Educational Technology

Timeline

12 months (Full Platform Development)

Tech Stack

Angular, Django, PostgreSQL, WebRTC

The Challenge

SkillDrop needed a robust platform to democratize education in Russia by connecting skilled professionals with eager learners. The challenges included:

  • Language barriers and localization needs for the Russian market
  • Secure payment processing across multiple regions
  • Real-time video streaming for online classes
  • Complex scheduling across different time zones
  • Building trust between instructors and students

Our Solution

We created a comprehensive educational marketplace with features designed for the global market:

Live Streaming Platform

WebRTC-based video streaming with screen sharing and interactive whiteboards

Advanced Search & Matching

AI-powered instructor matching based on skills, ratings, and learning preferences

Multi-currency Payments

Secure payment processing supporting rubles and international currencies

Review & Rating System

Comprehensive feedback system building trust in the community
